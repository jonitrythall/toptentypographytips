Use Hanging Quotes

Avoid having opening quotation marks that are vertically flush with a block of text. Instead, hang the quotes in the margin. Without hanging quotes the text becomes interrupted and messes with balance.

Selecting Typefaces

When selecting a typeface always focus on the body text first. Be sure to keep brand objectives in mind, read and understand the copy, and have a good sense of what feelings the site or product is aiming to foster.

Hierarchy

Make sure your heading levels are visually ranked by their importance through the use of different colors and sizes. Also, be mindful that elements near each other visually will communicate a relationship between them.

Spacing

Make sure all opportunities of vertical spacing throughout a page are consistent by setting a line-height as a baseline and then using multiples of this value.

Be mindful of using generous white space, or negative space, around elements to enhance readability, comprehension, and instill a feeling of calmness.
